Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

Market Pulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you access our website and Telegram channel.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. This includes information such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Click Data: We track when users click on links to our Telegram channel for conversion tracking purposes.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our website and content
  • Understand user preferences and behavior
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts
  • Comply with legal obligations

Telegram Channel

When you join our Telegram channel, you are subject to Telegram's privacy policy. We do not have access to your personal Telegram information beyond what you choose to share in the channel. All interactions within the Telegram channel are governed by Telegram's terms of service and privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and tracking
  • Google Ads: For conversion tracking
  • Telegram: For content delivery

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect information as described in their respective policies.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and tracking purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we collect about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Unsubscribe from our Telegram channel at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Telegram channel.
